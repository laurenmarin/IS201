Marketing Jobs and Salary


  1. Marketing Coordinator
    • A marketing coordinator assists with marketing campaigns, manages social media accounts, and creates marketing materials. The median annual salary for a marketing coordinator is around $47,000
  2. Marketing Specialist
    • A marketing specialist creates and executes marketing strategies, conducts market research, and manages advertising campaigns. The median annual salary for a marketing specialist is around $63,000.
  3. Marketing Manager
    • A marketing manager develops marketing plans and strategies, oversees the marketing team, and manages budgets. The median annual salary for a marketing manager is around $135,000.
  4. Digital Marketing Specialist
    • A digital marketing specialist creates and implements digital marketing campaigns, manages social media accounts, and analyzes digital data. The median annual salary for a digital marketing specialist is around $63,000.
  5. Social Media Manager
    • A social media manager creates and manages social media content, tracks social media metrics, and engages with social media followers. The median annual salary for a social media manager is around $51,000.
  6. Market Research Analyst
    • A market research analyst conducts market research, analyzes data, and presents findings to stakeholders. The median annual salary for a market research analyst is around $65,000.
